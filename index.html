Welcome to Pawtraits

At Pawtraits, We understand that pets are members of the family, so we believe they deserve purrfect portraits to showcase them in all their glory!

We have a London studio for photoshoots, and can also join you on location anywhere in the UK. Our experienced photographers are skilled at working with animals and know just how to capture your pets at their best!

We specialise in cats, dogs, birds and bunnies but have also photographed horses, cows, ferrets and lizards! Have a look at our gallery to view more.
